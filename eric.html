Meet Eric

Eric Paul


President

Eric Paul is a proud member of the Din√© (Navajo) and Zapotec nations. He was raised by his father in the Mission District of San Francisco, California, where he was surrounded by art, music and community. During his young adult years Eric spent his time travelling and learning from different Indigenous communities across Turtle Island. The peyote has always been a part of his life and continues to help him grow. Through the medicine he has learned to put together traditional instruments and beadwork. He currently resides in Oregon where he is raising his two daughters with his lovely wife.
